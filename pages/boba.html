Boba Tea

In Asia, bubble tea cafes are everywhere. They’re on every corner, and I’m starting to see more of them take over the scene here in the U.S. I think it’s wonderful that it’s becoming so popular here. But what you didn’t know about this sublime tea drink is that it’s easy to make at home!

Ingredients:

  • Tapioca pearls
  • Black tea
  • Brown sugar syrup
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Cook the tapioca pearls.
  2. Marinate the tapioca pearls in syrup.
  3. Brew a very strong tea.
  4. Add the tapioca pearls and syrup into a glass.
  5. Pour in the brewed tea.
  6. Finish it up with milk.
